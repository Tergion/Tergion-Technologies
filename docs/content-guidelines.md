# Content Guidelines

- Use serious B2B systems language.
- Position Tergion Technologies as a business systems, automation, AI workflow, and CRM infrastructure company.
- Do not make the company sound like a cheap agency.
- Do not overhype AI or imply autonomous replacement of professional judgment.
- Do not make unsupported statistics or fake performance claims.
- Do not fake testimonials, client logos, Google reviews, or case studies.
- Do not claim guaranteed revenue or lead increases.
- Prefer `designed to improve` over guaranteed-outcome language.
- Keep GoHighLevel as one possible implementation tool, not the company identity.
- Emphasize visibility, handoffs, review points, and business control.
